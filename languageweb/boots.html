Why?

While foreign language ability is an incredibly useful skill, it comes at a cost and not all of the benefits are equal. Some languages have few practical uses or native speakers, becoming less useful and more difficult to maintain throughout my life.

Without a concrete reason to learn, my initial fascination will fizzle out before I'm fluent. So until I move to a foreign country or make a foreign-speaking friend, learning about language learning will be more of a hobby than actual language learning.
